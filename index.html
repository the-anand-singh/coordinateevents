We are a full service event management company offering turnkey event planning and organizing services for family and corporate events, operating throughout India. We started with a purpose to provide one-stop solutions for all your event-related worries. Coordinate Events specializes in making your celebrations memorable. We have managed to convert ourselves into a planning powerhouse to handle all your needs – starting from catering, decoration, photography, videography and assemble them all together for your big day. We have an amazing partner network of vendors who cater to all your needs and that help you as clients to get better pricing and save money, but still get what you desire for your celebrations of special moments. We have transformed ourselves in to a center for creativity in designing and do not shy away from introducing bold new concepts, which appeal to the modern Indian family, especially the bride and groom. We believe every celebration has a story to tell… what will your’s say?
