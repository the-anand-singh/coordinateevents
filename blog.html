Hatsoff Events unveils it's new corporate identity, launches Coordinate Events

Coordinate Events, an event management company, today announced a major re-branding to reflect the company’s evolution encompassing nationwide business. Previously known as Hatsoff Events, based out of Kolkata operated only in the eastern region of India. After successfully working in the region for 2 years and making a name for itself, the co-founders have now taken a decision to expand the horizon. They want to spread their presence in other parts of the country. In terms of line of business, Coordinate Events will now focus on corporate events alongside social events. The consolidated capabilities of the co-founders and their trusted vendors provides re-imagined space in terms of governing the scope of business. The re-branding includes a top-to-bottom redesign of the company’s website, logo, graphics, communications and correspondence. Coordinate’s new brand assets include a simplified, double-tone logo, along with a new website and other visual branded communications.
